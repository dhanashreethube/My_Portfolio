About Me

A glimpse into my world

Learn more about who I am, what I do, and what inspires me

My journey started a little differently. After completing my B.E. in Electronics & Telecommunication, I prepared for the UPSC Engineering Services exam — one of the most rigorous technical examinations in the country. That chapter shaped how I think: it taught me the discipline to tackle hard, long-term challenges, stay committed under pressure, and trust the process even when results take time.

Eventually, I found my way into software through a postgraduate program at C-DAC, Pune — and it felt like the right fit from day one.

Since then, I've been working as a Full Stack Developer at Trinamix. When I'm not coding, you'll find me travelling, painting, or picking up something new in tech.
